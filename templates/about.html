Who are you?

I am Yuwen Lu, currently a Junior student from School of Software Technology, Dalian University of Technology.


Why did you decide to make this web app?

This is the project for my optional course Open Source Software Basics. We are asked to make something with python, so I decided to use the famous python web microframework Flask to make this web app.


Why a Conference Assistant?

I served as the President of our campus's Model United Nations Association during the past year.
When we were organizing campus-wide conferences, I found out that there's no such a cross-platform MUN conference hosting software on the market or on the internet, so I decided to make a web app for this purpose on my own.
I have learned much through MUN and I really appreciate the opportunities our university's MUN Association has provided with me. I'm glad that I could give back to the community in this way.
